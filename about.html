about


Gōtemilk is a brand, popup, and internet store. My goal is to create & design content that resonates with my community & species.

At my popups we play videogames & watch vintage movies. Always free to play and there's always competition. Check the schedule for what's coming up next or visit me at the Downtown Grower’s Market and Railyards any 1st or 3rd weekend of the month this summer & fall.

I was born and raised in Carlsbad, New Mexico. I moved to Albuquerque seven years ago and I moved Downtown two years ago. I've done popups by the name Gōte since 2016, Gōtemilk was officially started in the fall of 2018.
